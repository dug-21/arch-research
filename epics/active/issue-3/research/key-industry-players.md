# Key Players in the Payments Industry

## Card Networks and Schemes

### Global Card Networks

#### **Visa Inc.**
- **Market Position**: Largest payment network globally
- **Transaction Volume**: 192.5 billion transactions (2023)
- **Payment Volume**: $14.8 trillion
- **Geographic Reach**: 200+ countries and territories
- **Key Products**:
  - Visa Direct (push payments)
  - Visa B2B Connect (cross-border)
  - CyberSource (payment management)
- **Technology Focus**: Tokenization, contactless, blockchain

#### **Mastercard**
- **Market Position**: Second-largest global network
- **Transaction Volume**: 140 billion transactions
- **Payment Volume**: $8.6 trillion
- **Key Innovations**:
  - Mastercard Send (disbursements)
  - Open Banking Hub
  - Digital ID solutions
- **Strategic Direction**: Beyond payments into data analytics

#### **American Express**
- **Business Model**: Closed-loop network (issuer + acquirer)
- **Market Focus**: Premium cardholders, T&E spending
- **Payment Volume**: $1.4 trillion
- **Unique Value**: Rich transaction data, loyalty programs
- **B2B Focus**: Virtual cards, supplier payments

#### **Regional Networks**
- **UnionPay (China)**: 9 billion cards issued, expanding globally
- **RuPay (India)**: 750+ million cards, government backed
- **Elo (Brazil)**: 140 million cards, local market leader
- **Mir (Russia)**: Domestic alternative to Visa/Mastercard
- **JCB (Japan)**: 150 million cards, Asia-Pacific focus
