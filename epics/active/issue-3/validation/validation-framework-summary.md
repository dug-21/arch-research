## Validation Criteria

### Critical Success Factors
1. **Completeness:** All 155 checklist items must be addressed

## Key Validation Areas

### 1. Stakeholder Coverage
Ensuring all payment ecosystem participants are documented:
- End users (B2C, B2B, International)
- Business stakeholders (Finance, Risk, Support)
- Technical teams (Architects, Developers, DevOps)
- External partners (PSPs, Banks, Regulators)

### 2. Process Completeness
Validating all payment flows are documented:
- Authorization flows
- Settlement processes
- Refund/reversal handling
- Tokenization
- Recurring payments
- Multi-currency support

### 3. Architecture Soundness
Verifying technical architecture documentation:
- System context (C4 Level 1)
- Container architecture (C4 Level 2)
- Component details
- Data models
- Security architecture
- Integration patterns

### 4. Compliance Coverage
Ensuring regulatory requirements are met:
- PCI-DSS (12 requirements)
- PSD2/SCA (Strong Customer Authentication)
- GDPR (Data privacy)
- AML/KYC (Anti-money laundering)
- Regional regulations

### 5. Operational Readiness
Validating operational documentation:
- Monitoring procedures
- Incident response plans
- Disaster recovery
- Performance benchmarks
- Maintenance procedures
