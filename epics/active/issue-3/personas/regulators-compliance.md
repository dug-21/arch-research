# Regulatory and Compliance Personas in Payment Systems

## Overview
Regulators and compliance officers ensure the payment ecosystem operates safely, fairly, and in accordance with laws. They protect consumers, maintain financial stability, and prevent financial crimes.

## Regulatory Journey Mapping

### New Regulation Implementation (Central Bank)
1. **Issue Identification**: Market problem recognized
2. **Stakeholder Consultation**: Industry feedback gathered
3. **Policy Development**: Rules drafted
4. **Impact Assessment**: Economic analysis conducted
5. **Public Comment**: Feedback period opened
6. **Final Rules**: Regulations published
7. **Implementation**: Phased enforcement begins
8. **Monitoring**: Compliance tracking initiated

### Compliance Program Development (Fintech)
1. **Regulatory Mapping**: Requirements identified
2. **Gap Analysis**: Current state assessed
3. **Program Design**: Controls developed
4. **Technology Selection**: Tools evaluated
5. **Implementation**: Processes deployed
6. **Testing**: Effectiveness validated
7. **Monitoring**: Ongoing compliance tracked
8. **Optimization**: Continuous improvement
