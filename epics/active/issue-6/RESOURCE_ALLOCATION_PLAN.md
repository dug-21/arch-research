### Resource Management Framework

#### Allocation Authority Matrix
| Resource Type | Budget Range | Primary Authority | Approval Required | Escalation Level |
|---------------|--------------|-------------------|-------------------|------------------|
| Core Team Adjustments | <$5,000 | Project Manager | Architecture Lead | Executive Sponsor |

#### Change Control Process
**Resource Change Request Procedure**:
1. **Request Initiation**: Document resource change requirement and justification
2. **Impact Analysis**: Assess timeline, budget, quality, and risk implications
3. **Stakeholder Review**: Gather input from affected team members and stakeholders
4. **Approval Workflow**: Route through appropriate authority levels
5. **Implementation**: Execute approved changes with communication and documentation
6. **Monitoring**: Track effectiveness of resource changes

**Change Control Timeline**:
- Minor adjustments: 24-48 hours processing
- Moderate changes: 3-5 business days
- Major reallocations: 7-10 business days
- Emergency changes: Immediate approval with post-implementation review
