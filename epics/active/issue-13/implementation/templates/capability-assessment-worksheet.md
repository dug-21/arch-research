## Section C: Maturity Assessment

### C.1 Current Maturity Level
For each level, check all criteria that are met:

**Level 1 - Initial (Ad Hoc):**
☐ Capability exists but is not formally managed
☐ Success depends on individual efforts
☐ Processes are unpredictable and poorly controlled
☐ Limited documentation exists
☐ Reactive approach to issues

**Level 2 - Managed (Repeatable):**
☐ Basic processes are established and repeatable
☐ Some performance measurement exists
☐ Project-level management is practiced
☐ Basic training is provided
☐ Some documentation and standards exist

**Level 3 - Defined (Proactive):**
☐ Processes are well-defined and standardized organization-wide
☐ Regular performance monitoring occurs
☐ Formal training programs exist
☐ Systematic process improvement activities
☐ Proactive issue identification and resolution

**Level 4 - Quantitatively Managed (Measured):**
☐ Statistical process control is used
☐ Performance is predictable and quantitatively measured
☐ Quantitative quality goals are established
☐ Process capability is understood and controlled
☐ Data-driven decision making is standard

**Level 5 - Optimizing (Innovative):**
☐ Focus on continuous process improvement
☐ Innovation in capability delivery is common
☐ Technology is leveraged for optimization
☐ Industry leadership position is maintained
☐ Performance consistently exceeds targets
