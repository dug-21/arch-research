## 1. STAKEHOLDER REVIEW MATRIX

### 1.1 Review Stakeholder Categories

| Stakeholder Type | Review Focus | Authority Level | Timeline |
|-----------------|--------------|-----------------|----------|
| **Executive Sponsor** | Strategic alignment, ROI, business impact | Final approval | 2-3 days |
| **Technical Lead** | Technical feasibility, architecture quality | Technical approval | 2-3 days |
| **Business Owner** | Requirements alignment, business value | Business approval | 2-3 days |
| **Project Manager** | Timeline, resources, deliverability | Process approval | 1-2 days |
| **Security Officer** | Security compliance, risk assessment | Security approval | 2-3 days |
| **Finance Representative** | Cost validation, budget alignment | Financial approval | 1-2 days |
| **End User Rep** | Usability, business process fit | User acceptance | 2-3 days |
| **IT Operations** | Operational feasibility, support model | Operations approval | 1-2 days |
| **Quality Assurance** | Quality standards, testing approach | QA approval | 1-2 days |
| **Compliance Officer** | Regulatory requirements, audit readiness | Compliance approval | 2-3 days |

### 1.2 Review Assignment Matrix

| Document Type | Primary Reviewers | Secondary Reviewers | Approval Required |
|---------------|-------------------|-------------------|------------------|
| **Current State Assessment** | Technical Lead, Business Owner | IT Operations, End User Rep | Technical + Business |
| **Gap Analysis** | Business Owner, Project Manager | End User Rep, QA | Business + Process |

## 4. ESCALATION AND CONFLICT RESOLUTION

### 4.1 Review Dispute Resolution Process

```
STEP 1: Direct Resolution (1-2 days)
- Author and reviewer discuss directly
- Document resolution or impasse
- Escalate if no agreement

STEP 2: Technical/Business Lead Review (1-2 days)
- Appropriate lead mediates discussion
- Technical issues → Technical Lead
- Business issues → Business Owner
- Document decision and rationale

STEP 3: Project Manager Arbitration (1 day)
- PM reviews all positions and evidence
- Makes binding decision for project
- Documents decision and process impact

STEP 4: Steering Committee Review (1-2 days)
- Only for strategic/high-impact disputes
- Full committee review and decision
- Final authority for project scope
```

### 4.2 Review Quality Issues

```
QUALITY ESCALATION MATRIX:

Minor Issues:
- Reviewer works directly with author
- 1-2 day resolution target
- No formal escalation required

Major Issues:
- QA Lead involvement required
- 3-5 day resolution target
- PM notification required

Critical Issues:
- Immediate escalation to project leadership
- 1 day resolution target
- Stakeholder notification required
- Potential project impact assessment
```

## 5. REVIEW METRICS AND CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT

### 5.1 Review Performance Metrics

```
REVIEW EFFECTIVENESS METRICS:

Efficiency Metrics:
- Average review cycle time
- First-pass approval rate
- Review completion rate
- On-time submission percentage

Quality Metrics:
- Issue detection rate
- Issue resolution time
- Review accuracy (validated post-implementation)
- Reviewer satisfaction scores

Process Metrics:
- Escalation frequency
- Review conflict rate
- Template usage compliance
- Process improvement suggestions
```

### 5.2 Continuous Improvement Process

```
QUARTERLY REVIEW PROCESS IMPROVEMENT:

1. Metrics Analysis
   - Review performance data
   - Identify trends and patterns
   - Benchmark against targets

2. Stakeholder Feedback
   - Survey reviewers and authors
   - Identify pain points
   - Collect improvement suggestions

3. Process Updates
   - Update templates based on feedback
   - Revise procedures for efficiency
   - Enhance training materials

4. Communication
   - Share improvements with stakeholders
   - Provide updated templates and training
   - Monitor adoption and effectiveness
```
