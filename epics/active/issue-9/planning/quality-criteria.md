## 4. STAKEHOLDER REVIEW PROCESS

### 4.1 Review Stakeholders
**Primary Reviewers:**
- [ ] Business Sponsor (Strategic alignment)
- [ ] Technical Lead (Technical feasibility)
- [ ] Project Manager (Implementation viability)
- [ ] Security Officer (Security compliance)
- [ ] Finance Representative (Cost validation)

**Secondary Reviewers:**
- [ ] End User Representatives
- [ ] IT Operations Team
- [ ] Quality Assurance Team
- [ ] Change Management Team

### 4.2 Review Cycle Process
1. **Initial Review**: 5 business days
2. **Feedback Consolidation**: 2 business days
3. **Revision Period**: 3 business days
4. **Final Review**: 2 business days
5. **Approval Decision**: 1 business day

### 4.3 Review Criteria Template
```
STAKEHOLDER REVIEW FORM
Document: [Document Name]
Reviewer: [Name and Role]
Review Date: [Date]

ASSESSMENT AREAS:
□ Content Completeness (1-5 scale)
□ Technical Accuracy (1-5 scale)
□ Business Relevance (1-5 scale)
□ Implementation Feasibility (1-5 scale)
□ Risk Coverage (1-5 scale)

SPECIFIC FEEDBACK:
[Detailed comments]

RECOMMENDATION:
□ Approve as-is
□ Approve with minor revisions
□ Requires major revisions
□ Reject - fundamental issues

SIGNATURE: [Name, Date]
```

## 5. RISK ASSESSMENT METHODOLOGY

### 5.1 Risk Categories
**Technical Risks:**
- [ ] Technology compatibility issues
- [ ] Performance degradation during migration
- [ ] Data integrity concerns
- [ ] Integration complexity
- [ ] Security vulnerabilities
- [ ] Scalability limitations

**Business Risks:**
- [ ] User adoption challenges
- [ ] Business process disruption
- [ ] Stakeholder resistance
- [ ] Budget overruns
- [ ] Timeline delays
- [ ] Competitive disadvantage

**Operational Risks:**
- [ ] Resource availability
- [ ] Skill gaps in team
- [ ] Third-party dependencies
- [ ] Regulatory compliance
- [ ] Change management failures
- [ ] Support and maintenance issues

### 5.2 Risk Assessment Scale

| Level | Impact | Probability | Score | Response Strategy |
|-------|--------|-------------|-------|------------------|
| Critical | High | High | 9-10 | Immediate mitigation required |
| High | High | Medium | 7-8 | Active mitigation planning |
| Medium | Medium | Medium | 5-6 | Monitor and prepare contingency |
| Low | Low | Low | 1-4 | Accept with documentation |

### 5.3 Risk Mitigation Framework
- **Prevention**: Measures to prevent risk occurrence
- **Detection**: Early warning systems and indicators
- **Response**: Immediate actions when risk materializes
- **Recovery**: Plans to restore normal operations
- **Communication**: Stakeholder notification protocols
