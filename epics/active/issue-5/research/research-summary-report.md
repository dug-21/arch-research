## Executive Summary of Findings

### Primary Methodologies Identified:

#### 🏗️ APQC Process Classification Framework (PCF)
- **Market Leader**: Most widely adopted globally (80% of Global 50 companies)
- **Current Version**: 7.3 (May 2022)
- **Structure**: 5 hierarchical levels (Categories → Process Groups → Processes → Activities → Tasks)
- **Strengths**: Benchmarking capabilities, industry standardization, BPMN integration

#### 🏛️ TOGAF Framework
- **Latest Version**: TOGAF 10 (2022)
- **Architecture**: BDAT domains (Business, Data, Application, Technology)
- **Focus**: Enterprise architecture alignment and digital transformation
- **2025 Enhancement**: Stronger agile methodology integration

#### ⚙️ BPMN 2.0 Integration
- **Standardization**: Visual process modeling notation
- **Integration**: Direct mapping with APQC PCF categories
- **Applications**: Cross-industry process modeling and automation
