## Risk Assessment

### Top 5 Risks & Mitigations

**1. Market Timing Risk (Too Early for Agentic Development)**
- **Probability:** 20-30%
- **Impact:** High (delays revenue, requires pivot)
- **Mitigation:** Phase 1 validates demand before platform investment, quick pivot to enhanced CI/CD consulting
- **Fallback:** Extend Phase 1 services, partner with established IDP vendors

**2. AI Reliability Risk (Hallucinations, Prompt Injection)**
- **Probability:** 40-50%
- **Impact:** Medium (requires human oversight, slower than fully autonomous)
- **Mitigation:** Mandatory human review, automated testing, gradual autonomy increase
- **Fallback:** Human-in-the-loop remains standard, position as "AI-assisted" not "AI-autonomous"

**3. Sales Cycle Risk (Longer Than Expected)**
- **Probability:** 30-40%
- **Impact:** Medium (cash flow challenges)
- **Mitigation:** Target platform engineering teams (shorter cycle), freemium Phase 1 services, partner channels
- **Fallback:** Bridge financing, extend Phase 1 runway

**4. Talent Acquisition Risk (Hard to Find AI + Security + Platform Engineers)**
- **Probability:** 50-60%
- **Impact:** Medium (slower delivery)
- **Mitigation:** Remote-first, competitive comp, equity, contract specialists
- **Fallback:** Offshore team for non-critical components, partnerships with consulting firms

**5. Regulatory Risk (AI Regulations Impact Business Model)**
- **Probability:** 15-25%
- **Impact:** High (requires redesign)
- **Mitigation:** Compliance-ready from day 1, engage regulators, legal counsel
- **Fallback:** Serve non-regulated industries, adjust platform to compliance requirements
