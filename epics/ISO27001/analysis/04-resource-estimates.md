## 1. Internal Human Resources

### 1.1 Information Security Manager / Virtual CISO

**Role Description:**
- Overall ISMS strategy and implementation leadership
- Policy development and approval coordination
- Vendor security management
- Internal audit coordination
- External audit liaison
- Executive reporting and steering committee support

**Full-Time Option:**
- **Cost:** $120,000-$180,000/year (salary + benefits + overhead)
- **Pros:** Dedicated focus, full availability, long-term investment
- **Cons:** High cost for small organization, may not have sustained full-time workload post-certification

**Virtual CISO Option (RECOMMENDED):**
- **Cost:** $50,000-$80,000/year (0.3-0.5 FTE equivalent)
- **Typical Engagement:** 60-80 hours/month during implementation, 20-40 hours/month post-certification
- **Pros:**
  - 60% cost reduction vs. full-time
  - Access to experienced ISO 27001 expertise immediately
  - No recruitment time or risk
  - Flexible scaling (increase hours during critical phases, reduce post-certification)
  - Provider typically has team backup if primary vCISO unavailable
- **Cons:**
  - Not on-site full-time (but most work can be remote)
  - Shared resource across multiple clients
  - May need to transition to internal hire eventually

**Part-Time Contractor Option:**
- **Cost:** $75,000-$100,000/year (0.5 FTE)
- **Pros:** More availability than vCISO, less cost than full-time
- **Cons:** Still requires recruitment, limited expertise depth, no team backup

**RECOMMENDATION:** Start with **Virtual CISO** for first 12-18 months, evaluate need for internal hire at Month 12.

**Year 1 Budget:** $50,000-$80,000

### 1.2 IT/Security Analyst

**Role Description:**
- Security tool implementation and configuration
- Log monitoring and alert triage
- Access reviews and provisioning
- Vulnerability management

- Backup monitoring and testing

**Allocation Model:**

- **Pros:** No additional headcount, staff gains security skills
- **Cons:** Other IT work may suffer, may need to defer non-critical projects

**Option B: Hire Dedicated Security Analyst:**
- **Cost:** $70,000-$100,000/year (full-time)

- **Cons:** High cost for small organization, may be difficult to recruit

**RECOMMENDATION:** **Reallocate existing IT staff 0.5-0.7 FTE**, supplement with vCISO guidance. Consider MSSP for 24/7 monitoring only ($5,000-$10,000/month) if budget allows.

**Year 1 Budget:** $30,000-$50,000 (allocated existing staff)

### 2.2 ISO 27001 Certification Audit

**Stage 1 Audit (Documentation Review):**
- **Duration:** 1-2 days (remote or on-site)
- **Scope:** Review ISMS documentation for completeness and compliance
- **Cost:** $10,000-$20,000
- **Outcome:** Findings report, recommendation to proceed to Stage 2 or remediate

**Stage 2 Audit (Implementation Verification):**
- **Duration:** 2-3 days (on-site)
- **Scope:** Verify control implementation through interviews, evidence review, testing
- **Cost:** $15,000-$30,000
- **Outcome:** Certification decision (certified, minor non-conformities, major non-conformities)

**Total Certification Audit Cost:** $25,000-$50,000

**Variables Affecting Cost:**
- Organization size (number of employees)
- Number of locations (single site vs. multi-site)
- Complexity of technology environment
- Certification body selected (varies by geography and reputation)

**RECOMMENDATION:** Obtain quotes from 2-3 accredited certification bodies at Month 6-8. Select based on:
- Accreditation (ANAB, UKAS, etc.)
- Industry expertise (technology, B2B, non-profit)
- Audit approach (collaborative vs. adversarial)
- Cost and timeline

**Year 1 Budget:** $25,000-$35,000 (optimized, smaller organization)

## 3. Technology & Tool Investments

### 3.1 Essential Security Tools

#### Asset Management System
**Purpose:** Track all IT assets, software licenses, data repositories

**Options:**
- **Snipe-IT (Open Source):** Free, self-hosted, requires IT effort to maintain
- **Jira Service Management:** $10/user/month (20 users = $2,400/year)
- **ServiceNow CMDB:** $100/user/month (too expensive for SMB)

**RECOMMENDATION:** Jira Service Management (already widely used, good integration)

**Year 1 Cost:** $3,000-$5,000 (including initial setup)
**Ongoing Annual Cost:** $2,400-$3,600

---

#### Security Information & Event Management (SIEM)

**Purpose:** Centralize logs, detect security events, forensic investigations

**Options:**

**Cloud-Native (RECOMMENDED for SMB):**
- **AWS CloudWatch Logs + Insights:** $0.50/GB ingested + $0.005/GB stored
  - Estimated: 50-100GB/month = $600-$1,200/month = $7,200-$14,400/year
- **Azure Monitor + Sentinel:** $2.76/GB ingested
  - Estimated: 50-100GB/month = $4,140-$8,280/month = $16,560-$33,120/year (more expensive)
- **Google Cloud Logging:** $0.50/GB ingested
  - Similar to AWS pricing

**Open Source:**
- **ELK Stack (Elasticsearch, Logstash, Kibana):** Free software, requires infrastructure and management
  - Infrastructure cost: $200-$500/month ($2,400-$6,000/year)
  - Management effort: 0.2-0.3 FTE ongoing

**Commercial SIEM:**
- **Splunk Cloud:** $150/GB/month = $225,000-$450,000/year (way too expensive)
- **Sumo Logic:** $90/GB/month (still expensive)

**RECOMMENDATION:** **Cloud-native logging** (AWS CloudWatch, Azure Monitor, or Google Cloud Logging) based on existing cloud provider. Simplest, most cost-effective for small organization.

**Year 1 Cost:** $10,000-$20,000 (includes initial setup and 12 months)
**Ongoing Annual Cost:** $10,000-$15,000

---

#### Vulnerability Management

**Purpose:** Scan for software vulnerabilities, track patching

**Options:**
- **Qualys VMDR:** $2,000-$3,000/year (cloud-based, easy to use)
- **Tenable.io (Nessus):** $2,500-$4,000/year
- **OpenVAS:** Free (open source), requires infrastructure and expertise
- **Cloud Provider Native:** AWS Inspector, Azure Defender, Google Security Command Center (often included)

**RECOMMENDATION:** Start with **cloud provider native scanning** (free/low-cost), add **Qualys** if gaps exist.

**Year 1 Cost:** $2,000-$5,000
**Ongoing Annual Cost:** $2,000-$4,000

#### Data Loss Prevention (DLP)

**Purpose:** Prevent sensitive data exfiltration, detect policy violations

**Options:**
- **Microsoft Purview (formerly Microsoft 365 DLP):** Included with E5 license, or $15/user/month standalone
  - 20-50 users = $3,600-$9,000/year
- **Google Workspace DLP:** Included with Enterprise Plus, or add-on pricing
- **Symantec DLP Cloud:** $20-$40/user/year = $4,000-$12,000/year
- **Forcepoint DLP:** Similar pricing
- **No DLP (risk acceptance):** Rely on access controls and monitoring only

**RECOMMENDATION:** If using **Microsoft 365**, leverage Purview. If using **Google Workspace**, use native DLP. Otherwise, defer DLP to Year 2 (not critical for initial certification if access controls are strong).

**Year 1 Cost:** $5,000-$15,000 (if implemented)
**Optimized:** $0-$5,000 (leverage existing platform or defer)
**Ongoing Annual Cost:** $5,000-$10,000

- **Google Workspace MFA:** Included with Business and Enterprise plans
