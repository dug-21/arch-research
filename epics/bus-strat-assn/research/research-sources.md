## Research Methodology

This research was conducted using the GOAP (Goal-Oriented Action Planning) search methodology with the Goalie MCP tool, which employs validated search strategies, academic sourcing, and citation grounding to minimize hallucination and ensure accuracy. All searches were performed between October 29-30, 2025.

## Search Parameters

- **Search Mode**: Web and Academic
- **Model**: Perplexity Sonar Pro
- **Reasoning Engine**: Advanced Reasoning Engine (ARE) enabled
- **Output**: Structured markdown and JSON with full citations

## Data Limitations and Caveats

1. **Self-Reported Data**: Many statistics come from self-reported surveys with potential response bias
2. **Sample Sizes**: Vary by source (100-2,975 respondents)
3. **Geographic Focus**: Primarily U.S.-focused; international trends underrepresented
4. **Vendor Bias**: Platform comparisons may reflect vendor marketing; independent verification recommended
5. **Rapidly Evolving Field**: AI and technology trends changing rapidly; data may quickly become outdated
6. **Association Type Variation**: Not all findings apply equally to all association types (professional, trade, hobby, etc.)
