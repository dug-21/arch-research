## Research Gaps Identified

1. **Quantitative SMB-specific case studies**: Limited published data on SMB transformation outcomes vs. enterprise case studies
2. **Low-discipline team transformation paths**: Insufficient guidance on progressing teams with minimal process maturity
3. **Tool consolidation ROI**: Limited research on migration costs vs. benefits for legacy tool replacements
4. **Executive communication templates**: Few practical frameworks for translating technical transformation to business value
5. **Incremental automation prioritization**: Need more decision frameworks for SMBs with resource constraints
